About Me

I am a PhD candidate specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at the University of French Polynesia. My research focuses on developing automated systems for the quality assessment of Tahitian pearls, combining computer vision, deep learning, and traditional expertise to support French Polynesia's pearl industry.

My work bridges the gap between AI technology and a traditional Polynesian jewel, aiming to enhance the evaluation process while enhancing the valuation of the Tahitian pearl on the international market.
